SR Mobile Scroll Trigger CTA

Seven design options for the new mobile sticky scroll-trigger CTA on the LiverMD Smarter Reviews page. Each link below opens a full working copy of the live page with one option wired up — scroll behavior, tap-to-collapse, and all.

How to test

  • The modal doesn't appear at all until you scroll far enough that the #1 LiverMD listing first enters the viewport.
  • Once it's appeared, it stays on screen for the rest of the page: expanded (full card) whenever the #1 listing is completely out of view, and collapsed to just the header bar whenever any part of the #1 listing is visible on screen — including scrolling back up above it into the main article content.
  • Tap the colored header bar to manually collapse/expand it.
